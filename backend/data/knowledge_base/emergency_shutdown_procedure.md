## Section 1: High Risk Over-Limit Protocol
---

### Overview
Safety protocol for critical thresholds.
### Trigger Thresholds
- Voltage > 190V
- Vibration > 55 units
- Pressure < 50 PSI during load
### Recovery Procedure
Press emergency stop, isolate mains, consult electrical/mechanical lead.
